Must-Try Dishes — Matched to Your Dietary Needs
Pho Ga All 4
Phở Gà — Chicken Noodle Soup
Vietnam's national dish — clear aromatic broth with rice noodles and chicken. Eaten for breakfast. Always say "Pho Ga" (chicken) — "Pho Bo" is beef.
📍 Best in Hanoi · Days 4 & 6 · ~40,000–90,000 VND
Pho Chay Revanth
Phở Chay — Vegetarian Pho
Pure vegetarian version of pho with mushroom and vegetable broth. Available at most "quan chay" (veg) restaurants across all cities.
📍 Hanoi, Sapa, Da Nang · All days
Banh Mi Ga R·S·Sai
Bánh Mì Gà — Chicken Baguette
Crispy French baguette with chicken, pickled vegetables, fresh herbs and chili. CNN's world's best sandwich. Say "Banh Mi Ga" for chicken.
📍 Best in Hoi An · Day 7 · Banh Mi Phuong · ~20,000–40,000 VND
Banh Mi Chay Revanth
Bánh Mì Chay — Veg Baguette
Same iconic baguette but filled with tofu, pickled veg, herbs. Ask for "Banh Mi Chay" anywhere. Widely available across Vietnam.
📍 Everywhere · ~20,000–40,000 VND
Com Ga Hoi An R·S·Sai
Cơm Gà Hội An — Hoi An Chicken Rice
Hoi An's most iconic dish — shredded chicken over yellow turmeric rice with chili sauce. A signature local dish you can only truly experience here.
📍 Hoi An Old Town · Day 7 · ~50,000–80,000 VND
Cao Lau R·S·Sai
Cao Lầu — Hoi An Noodles
Chewy thick noodles unique to Hoi An — made using water from a 1000-year-old Cham well. Topped with char siu pork, crispy croutons, fresh herbs. Veg version available.
📍 Hoi An only · Day 7 · ~50,000–80,000 VND
Mi Quang R·S·Sai
Mì Quảng — Turmeric Noodles
Central Vietnam's iconic dish — thick turmeric noodles with chicken, peanuts and crispy rice crackers in rich concentrated broth. Da Nang's most beloved local dish.
📍 Da Nang · Days 7–9 · Quan Mi Quang Ba Mua
Banh Xeo All 4
Bánh Xèo — Sizzling Pancake
Crispy golden rice flour crepe — sizzles loudly when batter hits the pan. Chicken version for Rohith/Sanjay/Sai. Ask for "Banh Xeo Chay" for Revanth. Tear, wrap in lettuce, dip.
📍 Da Nang & Hoi An · Days 7–8
Goi Cuon All 4
Gỏi Cuốn — Fresh Spring Rolls
Soft rice paper rolls with chicken, herbs, vermicelli noodles, dipped in peanut sauce. Light and healthy. Veg version easily available everywhere.
📍 Everywhere · Great starter · ~30,000–50,000 VND
Ca Phe Trung All 4
Cà Phê Trứng — Egg Coffee
Strong Vietnamese coffee topped with thick whipped egg yolk and condensed milk — like a dessert in a cup. A Hanoi original you must try. Rich, sweet, utterly unique.
📍 Hanoi Old Quarter · Days 4 & 6 · ~30,000–50,000 VND
Ca Phe Dua All 4
Cà Phê Dừa — Coconut Coffee
Iced coffee blended with coconut cream — tropical, creamy and refreshing. Available at Cong Ca Phe chain (nationwide). Perfect for hot Da Nang days.
📍 Everywhere · Cong Ca Phe chain · ~40,000–60,000 VND
Bun Cha R·S·Sai
Bún Chả — Hanoi Grilled Pork
Hanoi's signature dish — grilled pork patties in tangy sweet broth with rice noodles and herbs. Famous after Obama ate it with Anthony Bourdain. Contains pork — Revanth skip.
📍 Hanoi Old Quarter · Days 4 & 6
💡
Key phrases: "Pho Ga" = chicken pho · "Chay" = vegetarian · "Không nước mắm" = no fish sauce · "Không thịt" = no meat · "Ít cay" = less spicy
